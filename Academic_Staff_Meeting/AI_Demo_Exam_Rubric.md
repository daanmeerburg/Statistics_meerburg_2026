# AI Demo Exam Rubric

Total: 90 points.

## Question 1: Short conceptual questions (24 pts)

Six subquestions, 4 pts each.

- Likelihood vs probability density: 2 pts for likelihood as a function of parameters with fixed data; 2 pts for posterior/prior normalization distinction.
- CLT: 2 pts for approximate Gaussian statement; 2 pts for relevance to averages and experimental errors.
- Bootstrap: 2 pts for advantage; 2 pts for limitation.
- Confidence vs credible interval: 2 pts for repeated-sampling coverage; 2 pts for posterior-probability interpretation.
- Evidence: 2 pts for prior-averaged likelihood; 2 pts for Bayes-factor model comparison.
- MCMC diagnostics: 2 pts per valid diagnostic with interpretation.

## Question 2: Computational/theory problem (42 pts)

- MLE derivation: 7 pts.
- Gaussian posterior derivation: 9 pts.
- Prior-width limits: 8 pts.
- Evidence derivation: 10 pts.
- Qualitative Occam penalty explanation: 8 pts.

## Question 3: Code question (24 pts)

- Comment placement: 12 pts, 2 pts each. Correct order: A, B, C, D, F, G.
- Quantity being estimated: 5 pts.
- Explanation of replacement resampling and interval: 7 pts.
